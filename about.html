About Us

About Us

FrenchShaala is a French language learning platform that began its journey in 2018 with offline classroom teaching. Over the years, it has grown into a pan-India French learning community, helping learners achieve fluency through structured, engaging, and practical methods.

We support students from different cities and backgrounds by offering well-designed courses, real-life language practice, and personalized guidance. Our mission is to make high-quality French education accessible, enjoyable, and effective for learners across India.

Our Vision & Social Impact

At FrenchShaala, we strongly believe that education has the power to create meaningful change. Along with teaching French, we aim to give back to society by supporting educational initiatives for underprivileged children and orphans, ensuring learning opportunities reach those who need them most.

FrenchShaala stands for quality education, meaningful learning experiences, and social responsibility. We focus on building confidence, communication skills, and cultural understanding in every learner.

Meet the Founder

Riddhi Shah, the founder of FrenchShaala, has always believed that French is more than just a language—it is a passion worth sharing. With years of teaching experience, she has guided over 150+ learners from diverse age groups ranging from 8 to 50 years.

As a certified DU FLE Trainer and DELF B2 certified professional, she combines strong academic expertise with hands-on teaching experience. Her focus is on building a solid foundation, encouraging real-life language usage, and making French learning enjoyable and approachable for every student.
